How long does the procedure. What is the recovery time?


Breast augmentation surgery typically takes one to two hours. Is commonly performed under anesthesia.

Recovery times can vary from person, to person. Most patients can typically go back to work within a week after the procedure. Its recommended to avoid activities for 4 6 weeks post surgery. Dr. Amr, who works exclusively at the Charme Day Surgery Centre in Dubai has introduced a " Rapid Recovery" approach where patients can start moving within an hour of surgery with minimal discomfort.

When it comes to breast augmentation like any procedure there are risks involved such as infection, bleeding, scarring and anesthesia related complications. Dr. Amr, a recognized expert with experience in performing over 6500 surgeries including complete breast reconstruction procedures ensures continuous care and guidance to minimize these risks.

Selecting the surgeon for breast augmentation is crucial. Its recommended to choose a board certified plastic surgeon with expertise in this field. Look for someone affiliated with organizations like the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS). Dr. Amr is a member of ASPS along with esteemed organizations such as the German DGPRAEC and the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons.

If you're concerned, about how breast augmentation may impact your ability to breastfeed...Many women can breastfeed after having breast augmentation surgery though the impact, on breastfeeding may vary depending on the approach. Incisions made beneath the breast fold or through the armpit are generally less likely to interfere with breastfeeding compared to incisions, around the areola. It's advisable to talk to your surgeon about your future breastfeeding intentions before undergoing surgery to reduce any risks or complications.
